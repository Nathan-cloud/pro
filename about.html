How we came to be
Prozlab was created from two terms. "Pro" stands for professional, while "Lab" stands for laboratory. We are a team of experts driven to transform the world by designing, creating, and delivering revolutionary technology products.
We remained unknown to the rest of the world until 2020, when it found its own home in our minds. But, as the year 2020 approached, we felt an unavoidable drive to make things happen. As a result, Prozlab was born.
We fused our knowledge, experience, deep domain expertise and love for innovation of delivering top tier tech products and services to the world from our remote lab of professionals.
